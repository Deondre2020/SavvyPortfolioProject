Greetings Ferguson Residents

Greetings Ferguson Residents: My name is Jacqueline Patton. I am a former paralegal, legislative assistant, and city clerk. During my years with both legislators, and city government I realized that voters did not have the necessary information in order to make a sound decisions in the voting process. I want to change all that. The Voter newsletter is to help keep residents informed of decisions that are made by their local government. While working in the legislator it occured to me that many people don’t understand the legislative process. How a bill becomes law, the process for which that bill takes in order to become law. Also, each year residents are asked to vote for certain individuals who want to hold political office. Yet, many of you know absolutely nothing regarding that candidate, other than what that candidate projects to you. I want to change all this by giving you information regarding that candidate, their background, and their understanding of the political process. You are asked to vote on judges every 2 years, yet the information received on how these judges run their courts are 0 to none. Why is it important to know our elected officials and the judges that will be serving the 21st Judicial Circuit. These decisions have the to impact your life and livelihood. The Voter is committed to bring you the best and most accurate information in order for you to make the best decisions when selecting individuals for elected office. Over the years I have stood by and watched voters elect individuals who had absolutely no understanding of the political arena. Why does this hurt us. If our elected officials are not abreast of the political arena, they are ill equipped to handle our needs or concerns. They are unable to reach out and get us the help we need. When electing a public official voters must understand they are electing someone to govern their community. You should want this person’s history of community service, background, as well as how they will your community if elected. The Voter hopes to enlighten you on propositions presented to you for your vote. There are times when not enough information is given in time for you to make a conscious decision on propositions. Again, The Voter hopes to change that. This first edition is simply to introduce you to The Voter. My hopes and dreams are that The Voter will become a local newsletter serving communities across the city and county of St. Louis. If you are a elected official and would like events or legislation, please feel free to contact me at (314) - 709-7786. Lets all have a clear understanding of the political process and the people who serve us. Sincerly, Jaqueline Patton Editor
